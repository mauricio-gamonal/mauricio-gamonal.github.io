Primordial cosmology

I study effective descriptions of the very early Universe, focusing on purely gravitational models of inflation and their observational consequences for the CMB and large-scale structure.

  • Higher-curvature gravity and Starobinsky-type models
  • Precision primordial power spectra (NLO, N2LO, N3LO)
  • Non-trivial initial states and primordial features

Quantum gravity & spinfoams

On the more fundamental side, my work focuses on Lorentzian spinfoam models and their semiclassical dynamics, with an eye toward operational notions of causality and boundary correlations.

  • Causal prescriptions and Toller functions for spinfoams
  • Large-spin asymptotics and semiclassical dynamics
  • Bridges to effective field theory and cosmology
